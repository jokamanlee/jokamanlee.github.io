Ways Of Seeing

Intro To Web Development


Josephine Lee


“Ways of Seeing” brings up a really interesting point in the beginning about how in the past, people had to physically move and go see what the wanted to, such as art, but now everything can come to us. Technology, for example the camera is able to capture images and allow for it to travel to us. However, what I never realized until watching this documentary is how this reproduction of this image changes the meaning of the original in many ways. To me, a reproduction, especially with the technology that we have these days is just a copy of the original, so the meaning wouldn’t change. However, the documentary goes on to explain the small changes that are made to reproductions that we don’t even realize sometimes, and these changes sometimes makes the original meaning change. I do agree with the idea that many things we see today are usually comprised of other people’s work, and many people would manipulate the meaning of such works to help support their own. There is so much manipulation that happens to original works, sometimes we don’t even realize it is happening, and I find this to be very true. Something that was also mentioned by John Berger is how our interpretations of many things is affected by our experiences, and this might hinder our ability to find the original meaning. However, the main point, which I agree with is many things we see today is arranged in a way to convey a certain meaning and this might not always be the original meaning, and how everyone should be aware of this.
