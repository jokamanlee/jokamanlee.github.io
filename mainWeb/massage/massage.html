The Medium is the Massage
By:Marshall McLuhan
The medium of the message impacts the scale of the message.
We are consumed and wrapped up by the medium.
The medium becomes extensions of our body parts.
It changes the way we think and interact with it.
A physical enclosure no longer means we won't
informed of the world around us.
We prefer and trust what we see over what we hear.
We focus on the content rather than the medium,
we don't realize the structural changes.
The medium has changed society, the way we communicate
and the way we interpret information.
